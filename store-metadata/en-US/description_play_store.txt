LONG DESCRIPTION Play Store (max 4000 chars):
Evolya'Fit is the mobile app connecting every athlete with their coach.

Your coach invited you to Evolya'Fit? Find everything here:

WEEKLY CHECK-IN
Complete your weekly check-in: weight, energy, sleep, notes. Your coach sees your progress in real time and adapts your programme accordingly.

DIRECT MESSAGING
Ask your coach questions directly in the app. Get advice, encouragement, and feedback without delay.

TRAINING PROGRAMME
View your personalised programme week by week. Each session details exercises, sets, reps, and rest times. Supersets are grouped so you can flow through without breaks.

GOAL TRACKING
Your monthly goals appear on your dashboard. Track your progress and stay motivated.

REAL-TIME NOTIFICATIONS
Get notified the moment your coach replies or updates your programme.

---
Evolya'Fit is exclusive to clients of coaches using the Evolya platform. If you haven't received an invitation, contact your coach.
